About Us

City Central Heating is a Wakefield-based heating engineer company that offers a wide range of services, including boiler repair, gas appliance installation, repair and servicing. We are fully qualified and experienced, and we use only th highest quality parts and materials. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service, and we are always available to answer any questions you may have.

Our owner, Luke Clouth, has been working in the heating industry for over 15 years. He is a qualified gas safe engineer, and he is a member of the Gas Safe Register. He is also a member of the Institute of Plumbing and Heating Engineers.

We understand that your heating system is essential to your comfort and well-being, and we are here to help you keep it in good working order. We offer a range of services to suit your needs, and we are always happy to provide a free quote.
